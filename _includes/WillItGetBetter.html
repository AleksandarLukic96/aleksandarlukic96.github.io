Will It Ever Get Better?

There is no easy answer to this question. The parking situation in Copenhagen is complicated. Not just for the people trying to find parking, but also for the ones responsible for solving the infrastructural problems. Numerous actors are trying in their own way to make getting around the city easier.

In our research we have not touched upon the public transportation which is obviously also a big part of the potential solution. For a moment, consider a Copenhagen where public transportation is so established that private parking becomes irrelivant. Of course that scenario is still well into the future, so until then we recommend planning your trip. Conisder where you are going and if there is likely to be parking there. Use our or others' ressources to predict the occupancy in the area and if the odds are against you: use public transportation. The following city overview should help you get a better idea of the parking situation in the city and predict the chances of finding parking.

And if none of this works, buy yourself an eletric car and skip some of the hassle.
