Parking

Before diving into why parking seems to be difficult to find in Copenhagen, we should first ascertain whether parking opportunities have actually been diminishing. The idea is simple: if the the number of parking spaces are decreasing, parking should be harder to find.
So, let's looks at a simple graph of total street parking spaces in Copenhagen over the years.

From this figure, it seems like there should be more parking. However, for several reasons, simply having more parking spaces does not necessarily mean that it is easier to find parking. Maybe the increase in parking spaces is not keeping up with the increase in cars.
