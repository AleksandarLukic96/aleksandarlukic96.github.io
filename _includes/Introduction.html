Parking In Copenhagen

Talk to any car owner in Copenhagen and they'll talk your ear off about how difficult it is to park anywhere in Copenhagen, unless you own your own parking spot. Is this the reality or just complaints and bad planning? To answer that question, we need to look at the parking situation in Copenhagen and how it has changed over the years. Parking has always been a hot topic in Copenhagen, and it has been a source of frustration for many residents and visitors alike. The city has a long history of car ownership, and as the number of cars on the road has increased, so too has the demand for parking spaces.
